House Rules:
  • Please keep your microphones muted and your webcams turned off.
  • If you want to ask questions to the speakers, please write your question in the chat.
  • A moderator in the room will ask the questions on your behalf.
  • Please use the chat rationally and only for topics relevant to the conference.
