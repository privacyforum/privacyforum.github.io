Registration Tool

Registration and payment are handled by the University of Salzburg's event management system. The registration link points to the general event site, in which you need to navigate to the "registration" tab. To change the language of the registration site to English, please use the button in the top-right corner. You can proceed as a guest or register for the event management system.
