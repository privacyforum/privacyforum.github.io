Participation Fees

We offer two different types of tickets, all of which are subject to reduced early-bird rates until Sunday, July 19, 2026.

Ticket type Early bird Full rate
Regular ticket (brutto) 384 € 434 €
Supporter ticket (brutto) Get one ticket for yourself and sponsor a further student's APF admission. You may select the supported student yourself or leave nominations to us. Further details may be clarified via e-mail. 485 € 525 €

Subject to availability, we might be able to offer a discount for (PhD) students. If that is of interest to you, please contact nicola.leschke@plus.ac.at.
