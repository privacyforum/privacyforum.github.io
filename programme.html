Conference Programme

The APF program comprises consciously curated invited talks and keynotes by outstanding authority representatives, practitioners, researchers and other privacy stakeholders, complemented by presentations of scientific papers carefully selected by the PC and one legislation-oriented panel. Of the selected scientific papers, the program foresees 30 minutes for full and 20 minutes for short papers.

Together, these contributions provide comprehensive and multifaceted perspectives to this year’s theme “Ten Years of GDPR – Engineering the Future”. Contributions originate from – inter alia – law, technology, and social sciences, together stipulating the unique inter- and transdisciplinary exchange and discourse that APF is specifically known for.

The program is continuously being refined and completed, particularly with updated talk titles.

Opening Event

On the evening of September 8 – thus closing the traditional pre-APF IPEN event organized by the European Data Protection Supervisor, and at the same time welcoming all APF participants – the city of Salzburg and state of Salzburg jointly invite to a fine classical concert in the stunning marble hall (“Marmorsaal”) of Mirabell Castle. Drinks and small snacks will presumably be available to buy before the concert.

  • When: September 8, 18:00 – 19:00
  • Where: Mirabell Castle, Marmorsaal
  • How to get there: Organized walk from conference location or individual arrival
  • Sponsored by: City of Salzburg and state of Salzburg
