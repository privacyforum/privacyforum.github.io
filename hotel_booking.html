Hotel Booking

Accommodation information for APF 2026 participants.

Salzburg offers a broad variety of hotels across price ranges from down to earth to fine residence. Even though APF was carefully planned to not overlap with major events such as the classical Festspielzeit, rooms might nonetheless get pricey quite soon, especially for hotels close to the popular old town and the river Salzach.

Discounted Hotel Contingent

We therefore teamed up with Salzburg's local congress management organization Salzburg Congress to provide a contingent of discounted rooms from a list of hotels consciously curated for APF attendance.

Bookings from this contingent can be made via the Salzburg Congress booking page.

Book via Salzburg Congress

Alternatively, you may also look for hotels close to the conference venue yourself. In case of further questions, just get in touch via the contact page.
