Annual Privacy Forum

Ten years after the adoption of the GDPR, the EU legal framework remains the global cornerstone for empowering individuals. Yet, the challenges of the next decade look vastly different.

APF 2026 invites you to Salzburg on September 7-9 to discuss how law, technology, and practice can be integrated to address future challenges.
