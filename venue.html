Reaching Salzburg

Salzburg is located in the heart of Europe. Therefore, it is easily reachable by train, long distance bus and airplane. While Salzburg has a lot of direct connections, you can also travel via Vienna or Munich (Germany).

Access by Train

Salzburg main station is well-connected to the European neighbors. Moreover, it is both part of the Austrian train network and endpoint of the German train network. Mobility in Salzburg

Within Salzburg, the guest mobility ticket is granted for all overnight guests and gives you access to all public transport tickets within the entire province of Salzburg. The ticket allows travel on city and regional buses, suburban trains, regional and long distance trains, as well as micro public transport connections, ensuring sustainable, stress free mobility within Salzburg.
