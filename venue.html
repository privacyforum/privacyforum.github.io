Venue

APF 2026 will be hosted at Unipark Nonntal, one of the University of Salzburg's largest and most modern buildings.

Unipark Nonntal

Located close to the historic old town and at the foot of the famous Hohensalzburg fortress, Unipark Nonntal combines state-of-the-art event facilities with, depending on the weather, stunning rooftop views to historic buildings as well as the nearby countryside and mountains.

Most famous points of interest as well as hotels or a relaxing break by the Salzach river are also just a quick walk or a few bus stations away.

Conference Areas

APF will take place at the ground level for registration and the main hall, the basement for meals and coffee breaks, and presumably the rooftop terrace of the building.

For simpler orientation, the university provides an interactive 360 degree tour of the building here.
