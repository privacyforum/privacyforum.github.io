Call for Sponsorship

Annual Privacy Forum 2026 | 9-10 September 2026 | Salzburg, Austria

We invite organizations and institutions to support APF 2026 and engage with an interdisciplinary privacy community of researchers, policy makers, regulators, and practitioners.

Sponsorship helps us keep participation inclusive and ensure a high-quality conference experience.

Sponsorship Tiers

  • Platinum Sponsor
  • Gold Sponsor
  • Special Sponsor (supporting specific parts such as meals or social activities)

Platinum Sponsor

Best suited for: Organizations seeking maximum visibility and strategic presence.

Expected contribution: Highest financial support tier.

Benefits

  • Top logo placement on website and sponsor materials
  • Recognition in opening and closing remarks
  • Exhibition/visibility space at venue (subject to venue rules)

Gold Sponsor

Best suited for: Organizations seeking strong visibility with moderate commitment.

Expected contribution: Mid-level financial support.

Benefits

  • Logo placement on website and sponsor materials
  • Acknowledgment during conference announcements

Special Sponsor

Best suited for: Partners contributing products or services for a specific conference component.

Examples: Coffee breaks, meals, welcome reception support, printed materials, or technical/event services.

Recognition

  • Recognition as Special Sponsor for the supported activity
  • Logo placement on the website

Contact for Sponsorship

For sponsorship discussions and tailored packages, contact us at apf2026@easychair.org or via the Contact page.
