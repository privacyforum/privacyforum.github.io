Registration 2025

On-site and virtual participation details for the 2025 event.

On-site Participation

On-site participation in the Annual Privacy Forum 2025 is free of charge for registered participants, i.e. there are no registration fees.

If you plan to attend in person, please register following this link:

Virtual Participation

Virtual participation in the Annual Privacy Forum 2025 is free of charge, i.e. there are no registration fees.

There is no need to register for virtual participation!

Access the Conference

The conference video system can be accessed via the link below:

https://bbb.uni-frankfurt.de/rooms/9bx-4vm-cqw

House Rules:
  • Please keep your microphones muted and your webcams turned off.
  • If you want to ask questions to the speakers on site during a session, please write your question to the chat.
  • We will have someone in the room asking the questions to the speakers on your behalf.
  • Please use the chat rationally, and only on topics of relevance to the conference.

We hope you will enjoy APF 2025 online.
